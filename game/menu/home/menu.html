It looks like you're playing on http!

If you decide to add Cutie Clicker to your phone's home screen, please make sure you're adding https://cc.aideen.pw/ for (slightly) faster loading times.

If you wannya switch to https, feel free to navigate there and update your bookmarks. Note that in most cases, your game data won't carry over, which is why you don't get redirected automatically. If you want auto-redirection, just delete your http game data, and you'll get redirected automatically! It's a safety measure I put in to make sure you don't just randomly lose your game unless you delete it yourself.
